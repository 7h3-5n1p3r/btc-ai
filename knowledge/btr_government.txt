1. INTRODUCTION TO BTR GOVERNANCE

The Bodoland Territorial Region (BTR) is governed by the Bodoland Territorial Council (BTC), an autonomous body under the Sixth Schedule of the Constitution of India. BTC provides decentralized governance to ensure development, autonomy, and cultural preservation.

====================================================================

2. EVOLUTION FROM BTAD TO BTR

BTAD (Bodoland Territorial Area Districts) was the earlier administrative name.

In 2020:
- BTAD was renamed as BTR (Bodoland Territorial Region)
- Administrative powers were expanded
- Governance structure strengthened

====================================================================

3. BTC – OVERVIEW

- Autonomous council under Sixth Schedule
- Formed in 2003
- Strengthened in 2020 Accord

Main Objectives:
- Self-governance
- Development
- Cultural protection

====================================================================

4. BTC STRUCTURE – DETAILED

Total Members: 46
- 40 Elected Members
- 6 Nominated Members

Main Bodies:
- Legislative Council
- Executive Committee
- Administrative Secretariat
